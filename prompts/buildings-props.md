# STELLAR PRINCESSES — Building & Prop Prompts
# ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Pixel art building exteriors and interactive props for the game world.
Buildings are viewed from a slight 3/4 angle (not pure top-down) to
show height and depth. Props are pure top-down.

For art direction rules, see `art-direction.md`.
For palette reference, see `config.js` COLORS.

## Style Rules

- **Buildings**: 3/4 perspective, pixel art, clean outlines. Show
  front face + partial roof. Readable silhouette.
- **Props**: Pure top-down, 16×16 or 32×32 pixels. Must be
  instantly recognizable at small size.
- **Lighting**: Upper-left light source, consistent across all assets
- **Palette**: Max 12 colors per building, 8 per prop
- **Outline**: 1px near-black #1a1a2a on all edges
