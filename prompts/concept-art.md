# STELLAR PRINCESSES — Concept Art Prompts
# ═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Concept art for promotional use, design reference, and world-building.
These are NOT game sprites — they are illustrations, paintings, and
diagrams. Style is less constrained than the pixel-art sprite prompts.

For pixel-art game sprites, see the character files in this directory.
For art direction rules (palette, perspective), see `art-direction.md`.

## Style Guidance

**General style**: Digital painting, cinematic lighting, sci-fi fantasy.
Think Mass Effect meets classic JRPG — sleek futuristic technology
mixed with organic alien species and royal elegance.

**Color palette** (from `config.js`):
- Deep space: `#0a0a1a`, `#1a0a2a`, `#1a1a2a`
- Tech/metal: `#778899`, `#8899aa`, `#667788`
- Energy: `#ffcc33` (gold), `#44ddff` (cyan), `#aa44ff` (purple)
- Fire: `#ff4400`, `#cc2200`, `#ff8833`
- Water: `#2244aa`, `#44ff44` (Brimble's green), `#44aa66`
- Void/dark: `#1a0a2a`, `#220044`, `#0a0a1a`
- Skin tones: `#ffccaa` (human), `#ddaa88` (cat), `#44aa66` (frog),
  `#cc3333` (dragon scales)

**Composition**: Dramatic lighting, strong silhouettes, readable at
a glance. Characters should be instantly recognizable by shape alone.

## Prompt Engineering Notes for Concept Art

1. **For DALL-E / Midjourney**: These prompts are written for general
   AI image generators. They emphasize composition, color palette,
   and mood over pixel-specific constraints.

2. **For pixel-art generators**: Add "pixel art", "16-bit", "SNES
   style", "no anti-aliasing" to suppress smooth rendering. Remove
   "digital painting" and "cinematic".

3. **For consistency**: When generating multiple images in a series
   (e.g., the 4 boss arenas), use the same seed or style reference
   to maintain visual consistency across the set.

4. **Upscaling**: Concept art can be generated at any resolution.
   For best results, generate at the target aspect ratio at the
   highest available resolution. No nearest-neighbor upscaling
   needed — these are illustrations, not pixel art.

5. **Iterative refinement**: These prompts are starting points.
   Generate a first pass, then refine specific elements with
   follow-up prompts (e.g., "make the horns larger", "darker
   background", "more dramatic lighting from below").
